# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We expect all participation in this repository to remain respectful, precise, and constructive.

Examples of acceptable behavior include:

- respectful technical discussion
- clear and focused feedback
- good-faith suggestions for clarity
- professional disagreement on wording or documentation

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or abusive language
- impersonation or deceptive representation
- bad-faith legal argument spam
- intentionally misleading proposals

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove or restrict participation that harms the repository, community, or public clarity of the license materials.
