# Comparison

## AW Non-Commercial License 1.0 vs MIT

MIT allows commercial use. AW Non-Commercial License 1.0 does not.

## AW Non-Commercial License 1.0 vs GPL / AGPL

GPL and AGPL are open-source licenses and allow commercial use, subject to their own conditions. AW-NC is not open source and restricts commercial use.

## AW Non-Commercial License 1.0 vs No License

With no license, downstream users generally have no permission to use, modify, or distribute. AW-NC grants limited non-commercial permissions while reserving commercial rights.

## Best Fit

AW-NC is most suitable for authors who want public visibility and controlled adoption without granting blanket commercial exploitation rights.
